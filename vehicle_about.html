Bio

This is my 1991 Honda Civic DX project! It's actually my second 91 DX hatch - the first one was an abused mess when I got it, and half of the engine was in the trunk. In my previous Civic, I swapped in a D16A6, and most of the braking, suspension, exhaust, and sound system parts that now reside in this Civic. That one met its demise when it was hit by another car :(

For this project, I picked up the shell with a decent paint job and a couple B-series parts. I've swapped in all the parts from my old civic, and now I'm working on finishing up the JDM LS swap. In addition, I'm putting in as many parts as possible to prep for a future turbo build :) This has led my decisions in sourcing parts for the engine internals, fuel system, radiator & fan choice, and ECU & gauges.

Right now I'm just concerned with putting the car back together, but once I'm done with that I will be continuing to source turbo-prep parts including a catch can setup, upgraded fuel delivery parts, 4bar map sensor, baffled & tapped oil pan, oil cooler, etc.
